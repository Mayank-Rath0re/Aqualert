Synanceia is a genus of ray-finned fish belonging to the subfamily Synanceiinae, the stonefishes, which is classified within the family Scorpaenidae, the scorpionfishes and relatives.
Stonefishes are venomous, dangerous, and fatal to humans. They are the most venomous fish known.
In humans stings can cause intense pain, respiratory weakness, damage to the cardiovascular system, convulsions and paralysis, sometimes they can lead to death. However the exact mechanism is not yet fully understood.
